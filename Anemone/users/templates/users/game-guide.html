    Hello and Welcome to Phourtnight!
    Are you and all your roommates in a household and ready to play?
    Then let's get started!

    How the Game Works

    Overview

    Phourtnight is a collaborative game that may be played with any number of people who share a house. The purpose of the game is to accumulate points by completing tasks, levelling up, and obtaining lootboxes containing special rewards!

    Task Creation

    To begin, members of the household set tasks that must be completed. Your creativity is the only restriction on what they may be. Using the navigation bar on your left, navigate to the Tasks page.

    Then click on the Add Task button, add a name for the task, details about the task, and the day the task is due by. Finally, click the Create Task button to confirm.

    Now, every member of the household can see this task in the Uncalimed column on the task board. From here, every member can bid.

    Bidding

    Members bid the lowest number of points they are willing to complete a task for. After 24 hours the task is assigned to the lowest bidder. If no one bids, the task is assigned at random. This ensures that tasks that on one wishes to complete are done!

    Getting Points

    Once a member is assigned a task, the now claimed task will show in the To Do column of the task board. Once a member has started a task, only they can move the task from the To Do column to the In Progress column. This will help members keep track of the tasks currently being completed. Members have until the specified deadline to compelete tasks. Upon completion, the member who claimed the task can drag-and-drop the task into the Completed column and they are awared points accordingly!

    Please note that all tasks on the task board are visible to all members in the household. This ensures transparency and accountability amongst members.

    Points gained over the course of a two week period - a Phourtnight - will show in the leaderboard. The member with the most points at the end wins the Phourtnight and gets bragging rights!

    Kudos

    In addition, members can award other members points for actions they deem beneficial to the household, wheither that be acts of kindness, short notice favours, or anything else. The sky's the limit!

    To award kudos, navigate to the Leaderboard and click the 👍 next to the members name. Each click grants them +10 points. Click as many times as you believe they deserve.

    Think of kudos as sort of a "Like Button" for anything a member does.

    Levelling Up

    When a member gains enough points, they level up. A member who has levelled up will gain a lootbox which contains customisation options for a player's leaderboard name. The more a member levels up, the more lootboxes a member receives, and the more customisation options a member unlocks!

    Click on the Lootbox section of the navigation bar to go to the lootbox and click Roll. The noun-adjective combination that is rolled is yours to keep and to customise your leaderboard display name with.

    Finally

    There are some other pretty useful features to our app, like household notificatoins, but you have to play the game to find out!
